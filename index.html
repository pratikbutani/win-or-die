How to Play "Win or Die" Game

Objective:

Take the last ball from the basket to win the game!

Game Rules:
  • Players take turns removing balls from the basket.
  • On your turn, you must take at least 1 ball.
  • You can take up to the maximum number of balls allowed per turn (default: 5).
  • The player who takes the last ball wins the game.
Game Setup:
  • Enter your name
  • Set the total number of balls (5-100)
  • Set the maximum balls allowed per turn (1-20)
  • Choose the difficulty level
  • Select who starts first
Difficulty Levels:
  • Easy: Robot makes more random moves and misses opportunities.
  • Medium: Robot plays strategically most of the time.
  • Hard: Robot plays almost perfectly, using optimal strategy.
Strategy Tip:

Try to leave your opponent with a multiple of (maximum balls per turn + 1).
